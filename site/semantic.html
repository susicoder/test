Most Popular South Indian Dishes

IDLY

Idly is a well known south indian dish mad with rice floor and steamed in pot.Its served with chutney.

Dosa

Dosa is made with fermented floor.There are varities of dosa like onion,carrot and ghee dosa.

Poori

Poori is made with wheat floor and oil.To make fluffy poori rava is added.Best side dish is potato masala
